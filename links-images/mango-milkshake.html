Mango Milkshake Recepe

Mango shake is a very popular Indian summer drink during the mango season.

This mango shake recipe makes for a delicious and creamy shake. It quenches the thirst, keeps one full and is nutritious too.Mango milkshake also helps in weight gain .

This mango milkshake recipe is made with ripe sweet mangoes and whole milk. The combination of sweet mangoes and milk is one that can be consumed without any side effects as per ayurveda. note that the mangoes should be sweet and not semi-sweet or sour or tangy.

Ingredients

  • 2 alphonso mangoes - medium-sized or any sweet variety of mangoes
  • 1.5 cups milk or ¾ cup milk + ¾ cup water - chilled or at room temperature
  • 2 to 3 ice cubes - optional
  • 2 tablespoons sugar or honey or jaggery - optional

Instructions

  1. Rinse and pat dry 2 medium-sized mangoes. Peel and chop them. Add chopped mangoes in a blender jar.
  2. Add 1.5 cups milk.
  3. Also, add 2 to 3 ice cubes.
  4. Blend till smooth.
  5. Pour in glasses.
  6. Serve mango shake immediately.
